Dangers of Smoking

Smoking is linked to various health issues, including lung cancer, heart disease, respiratory problems, and more. Secondhand smoke also poses risks to non-smokers.

Quitting smoking can significantly improve one's health and reduce the risk of developing these serious conditions.

Take Action

If you or someone you know is struggling with smoking, seek help. There are resources and support available to assist in the journey to quit smoking.
